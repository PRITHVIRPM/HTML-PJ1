Frequently Asked Questions

What is the age limit for attending concerts?

Most concerts are all-ages events, but some may have age restrictions. Please check the specific event details for age requirements.

Can I bring my own food and drinks?

Generally, outside food and drinks are not allowed. However, some venues may have specific policies, so it's best to check beforehand.

What should I do if I lost my tickets?

If you lose your tickets, please contact customer support for assistance. Be sure to provide your order details for verification.

Is there parking available at the venue?

Most venues offer parking, but availability may vary. We recommend arriving early or considering public transportation options.

What time do doors open for the concert?

Doors usually open 1-2 hours before the concert starts. Please check the event details for specific times.
